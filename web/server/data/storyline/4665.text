"Lulu is a beautiful young woman who can seemingly work her charms on all of the men around her. She is currently being kept by the rich editor Dr. Ludwig Sch\u00f6n. She is just a plaything however and he is engaged to be married to Charlotte, a woman of his own class. He arranges for Lulu to appear in his son Alwa's musical revue and he too falls for all of her charms. When Dr. Sch\u00f6n and his fianc\u00e9e go to the theater, Lulu ensures that he is put in a compromising situation and the elder Sch\u00f6n feels he now must marry her, knowing full well it will ruin his reputation. On his wedding day, Dr. Sch\u00f6n reaches his breaking point. His actions cost him his life however and Lulu is convicted of manslaughter. She escapes with the help of her old cronies but together they begin a downward spiral."
