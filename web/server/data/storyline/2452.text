"The movie is an epic story of a young Genghis Khan and how events in his early life lead him to become a legendary conqueror. The 9-year-old Tem\u00fcjin is taken on a trip by his father to select a girl as his future wife. He meets B\u00f6rte, who says she would like to be chosen, which he does. He promises to return after five years to marry her. Tem\u00fcjin's father is poisoned on the trip, and dies. As a boy Tem\u00fcjin passes through starvation, humiliations and even slavery, but later with the help of B\u00f6rte he overcomes all of his childhood hardships to become one of the greatest conquerors the world has ever known."
